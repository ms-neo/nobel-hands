Empowerment of the Poor Families

Nearly 1 billion people still live in extreme poverty. We are helping families increase income and assets, facilitating women’s empowerment through education. In poor countries, 80% of all jobs for women are low level and low pay. That’s why your gift of an animal to a woman is so important.

No gift is too small to help feed families. All animals like cows, buffalo, sheep, goats and chickens hep reduce the poverty.

Our Goal

Noblehands is actively supporting these families to reduce hunger and poverty. The charity has bought buffalo heifers and goats to help support the families in need. In other words, this is a gift which grows poverty gets smaller in comparison.
