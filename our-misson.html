Our Mission

Noble Hands Charity’s mission is to work with families and communities to change the lives of unfortunate individuals who are stuck in the cycle of illiteracy and poverty.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide individuals financial support and set of skills so they could perform well in the society and live with dignity. We believe that this requires a model of partnership in which both the causes and effects of poverty and injustice are successfully addressed.

Our Purpose

Providing access to health facilities.
Address the environmental issues to improve the health of the locals.
Clean Drinking water for communities.
Schooling for children from poor background.
Promoting healthy activities, like sports, among youth with bleak future prospects.
